Introduction

Kgaile Digital Solutions (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Botswana Data Protection Act 18 of 2024 (“the Act”). This Data Protection Policy applies to all services we operate, including:

  • Tseleng (“Your Digital Road Coach”) — a WhatsApp-based driver theory test preparation service
  • Kgaile Digital Solutions — web and AI chatbot agency services

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Data Protection Policy.

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

Kgaile Digital Solutions acts as the data controller for all personal data collected through our services. As data controller, we are responsible for how your personal data is collected, stored, and used.

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, contact us at:

  • Email: hello@kgailedigital.co.bw
  • WhatsApp: +267 73 960 083
  • Website: kgailedigital.co.bw

2. What Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Tseleng — Driver Theory Test Prep

When you use Tseleng via WhatsApp, we collect:

  • Your phone number (used as your unique account identifier)
  • Your test session history, quiz responses, and progress scores
  • Weak topic areas and performance data to personalise your learning
  • Payment confirmation details (Orange Money / MyZaka transaction references)

We do not collect your full name, national identity number, or financial account credentials.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

In accordance with Section 19 of the Data Protection Act 18 of 2024, we process your personal data on the following lawful bases:

Complying with legal obligationsLegal obligationAll data as required by law

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data only for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • To provide and manage your Tseleng test preparation subscription
  • To track your quiz progress and personalise study recommendations
  • To verify manual payment submissions (Orange Money / MyZaka)
  • To improve our services based on aggregated usage patterns
  • To comply with our legal obligations under Botswana law

5. Data Storage, Security & Third-Party Processors

Your data is stored in encrypted databases using Supabase. We ensure that third-party processors we use are located in countries with adequate data protection standards, consistent with Section 74 of the Data Protection Act 18 of 2024.

Where data is processed outside Botswana, we rely on standard contractual protections. A copy of your data remains accessible within our systems.

You have the following rights in respect of your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retentions)
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time by replying STOP
